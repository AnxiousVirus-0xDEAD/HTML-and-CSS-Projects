Welcome to Cake Time!

Hi kids! This website will show you how to make a super yummy cake! Get ready to have some fun!

Yummy Stuff You Need

Here's what you need to make a cake. Ask a grown-up to help!

  • Flour (2 cups)
  • Sugar (1 cup)
  • Eggs (3)
  • Butter (1/2 cup)
  • Milk (1 cup)
  • Baking Powder (2 teaspoons)
  • Vanilla (1 teaspoon)
  • Sprinkles (Lots and lots!)

Make sure you have all the yummy stuff!

How to Make It!

Follow these steps with a grown-up. It's super easy!

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, baking powder in a big bowl.
  2. Melt the butter and add it to the bowl.
  3. Crack the eggs into the bowl. Ask a grown-up to help!
  4. Pour in the milk and vanilla. Mix it all up!
  5. Pour the mix into a pan. A grown-up will help with the oven!
  6. Bake it until it's golden brown. A grown up will know when its done.
  7. Add frosting and sprinkles! Yummy!

Mix everything together carefully.
